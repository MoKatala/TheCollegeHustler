What We Are

We come before
the accelerator.

Y Combinator, Techstars, Baobab Network and every other major accelerator requires a product, a team, and traction. Most college founders don't have any of that yet — and that's exactly who we're built for.

The College Hustler is a pre-accelerator venture platform for college students across Africa. We work with you at the earliest stage — from idea to MVP to your first revenue — and set you up for the funding rounds that come next.

We don't wait until you're ready. We make you ready.
