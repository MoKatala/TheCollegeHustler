Application Support

Want expert help turning an opportunity into a winning application?

Some founders already have a strong idea but need support packaging it properly. This support request flow helps students ask for hands-on help with pitch decks, written responses, founder profiles, and final submission preparation.

Deck and profile support Get help sharpening your startup summary, founder bio, traction points, and pitch deck.
Application writing help Useful for fellowships, grants, accelerators, and forms that require thoughtful written answers.
Time-saving option Ideal when deadlines are close and you need someone to help organise the application quickly.
Founder-friendly intake Students fill one simple form and your team can decide whether to take the request forward.
Open Support Form Best for grants, fellowships, scholarships, accelerators, and competition applications.
How it works
1Student fills the Google Form with the opportunity, deadline, and current materials.
2 Our team reviews the request and decides whether to offer support.
3Follow up manually with the founder and agree the next steps.
Good use cases
•Deadline is close and the founder needs help polishing the application fast.
•The founder has the idea but struggles to write strong answers or structure the story.
•The opportunity is competitive and the founder wants expert review before submitting.
